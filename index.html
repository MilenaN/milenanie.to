About Me

With over 20 years of design expertise, I am a seasoned Art Director and Graphic Designer with a proven track record across diverse industries such as entertainment, beauty, legal, financial, and more. I have left my creative imprint on brands like LEGO Games, Quigley Simpson, Glam Glow, Chip & Pepper, Intuit, and Warner Bros. Records.

Proficient in Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, and industry standards, I specialize in optimizing workflows, enhancing team efficiencies, and have a successful history of hiring and overseeing design teams. My skills extend to impactful 360 campaigns, dynamic video productions, Iconography, and Compositing. Passionate about transforming concepts into visually compelling designs, I bring strategic thinking to craft stories that resonate and elevate brands.
